🌐 Accident Detection & Alert System

Our system provides real-time accident detection and instant emergency alerts to keep you safe on the road. Get started in seconds to protect yourself and your loved ones.

🚑 How It Works

  • 🔑
    Quick Sign Up / Login: Use your email or a social account to get started instantly.
  • 📡
    Enable Location: Keep location services ON for accurate, real-time accident detection.
  • ✍️
    Report Accidents: Witness an accident? Report the location, severity, and add notes to alert others.
  • 🏥
    Instant Notifications: The system automatically alerts nearby hospitals and your emergency contacts.

In serious emergencies, always call 108 or your local helpline for immediate help.
